  • Improving and securing the app (analytics, fraud prevention, error debugging) — legal basis: legitimate interest.
  • Commercial communications about CarAI, if you opt in — legal basis: consent, withdrawable at any time.
  • Complying with legal obligations — legal basis: legal obligation.

4. CarAI Vision and images

When you use CarAI Vision, the image is sent to the Google LLC (Gemini) API, acting as a data processor under a Data Processing Amendment. Google processes the image solely to identify the vehicle; the result is returned to CarAI and the image is stored linked to your vehicle in your garage within the Supabase platform while your account is active.

Unless you give explicit consent, we do not use your images to train our own models or share them for commercial purposes. Since photos may capture licence plates or other people in the background, we recommend framing the vehicle and minimising the capture of third-party data.

5. Recipients and data processors

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with providers who deliver services to us under a data processing agreement (art. 28 GDPR), for example:

  • Database, storage and authentication — Supabase Inc. (infrastructure on Amazon Web Services).
  • Artificial intelligence (CarAI Vision) — Google LLC (Gemini API).
  • Authentication emails — Supabase Auth (verification and password-recovery emails).
  • Subscription and paywall management — RevenueCat Inc. (intermediary between the app and the stores; receives subscription status and identifiers needed to manage access).
  • Payment processing — Apple Inc. (App Store) and Google LLC (Google Play); the actual charge is handled by the respective store.

We may also share data with authorities when required by law.

6. International transfers

Some providers operate or transfer data outside the European Economic Area, primarily to the United States. The safeguards applied are:

  • Google LLC — Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, pursuant to art. 46(2)(c) GDPR.
  • Supabase Inc. / Amazon Web Services — SCCs together with additional technical measures (encryption and access controls).
  • RevenueCat Inc. — SCCs pursuant to art. 46(2)(c) GDPR for the transfer of subscription data to its US servers.

You may request further information or a copy of the safeguards by writing to support@joincarai.com.

Data is then deleted or anonymised. Data processed on the basis of your consent is kept until you withdraw it.

9. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data (including encryption in transit, access controls and data minimisation). No system is infallible; in the event of a security breach affecting your rights, we will act in accordance with arts. 33 and 34 GDPR.

10. Minors
