Frequently asked questions

What is a Key?
A Key unlocks a complete analysis: exact fair price, real marketplace comparables, personalised negotiation arguments, historical value chart and a watermark-free PDF dossier.
Do Keys expire?
No. Your Keys are yours forever. Use them whenever you need, with no expiry date.
What's included in a complete analysis?
Exact fair price with market range, real marketplace comparables, car-specific negotiation arguments, historical value chart and a professional PDF dossier.
What is a locked analysis?
The analysis runs for real, but the data appears blurred. You can see your answer exists — just one Key away. A new one unlocks every 90 days.
Any sign-up gifts?
Yes. When you create your account you get a free valuation gift (the car's fair price, without the full report). And every year on your birthday 🎂, a free Key.
